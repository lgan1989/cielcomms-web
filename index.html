Meet Our Founder

R

Ran Mo

Founder & Advisor

Ran is a seasoned consultant specializing in strategic communications, media relations and investor relations. Throughout her career, she has worked closely with senior stakeholders, providing trusted counsel to start-ups, large corporations and listed companies. She has supported more than 50 firms across different industries during her tenure at leading global PR consultancies.

Ran started Ciel & Co. Communications in 2025 with a clear focus of providing high-quality, results-driven communications counsel that helps knowledge-intensive companies achieve their business objectives.

Before transitioning into communications, Ran worked as an analyst covering fintech, cryptocurrency, sustainable investing and power markets. She also contributed to the Digital Strategy team at KGI, a leading Taiwanese investment bank.

Ran holds a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and Finance from the University of Hong Kong, and a Master's degree in Corporate Financial Management from SKEMA Business School in Paris, France.
